Dr Daniel Yon

Lecturer in Psychology

Goldsmiths, University of London

I am a Lecturer in Psychology at Goldsmiths, University of London . Before that I was researcher funded by the Leverhulme Trust (2018-2019) and a PhD student funded by the ESRC (2014-2018) at Birkbeck, University of London, where I remain an Honorary Research Fellow. Research in our lab uses a combination of techniques from experimental psychology and cognitive neuroscience to study how our brains build models of ourselves and the world around us, and how these models shape our perceptions, actions and decisions.

I am lucky to work with an exceptional set of students, colleagues and collaborators, and on this website you can see some examples of our work. If you are interested in learning more about what we do, working with us or would lke to join the group for a PhD, postdoc or research placement please feel free to get in touch.

I am also the early career committee member for the UK’s Experimental Psychology Society, and am happy to hear from early career researchers who’d like to discuss how the community can support you and your work.
